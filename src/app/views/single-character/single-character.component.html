Facts about the Character

His creation in comics

The Joker is a supervillain, who came to be in mainstream comic books back in the 1940s and was created by Bill Finger, Bob Kane (the creator of the Batman) and Jerry Robinson.

His Abilities

The Joker doesn’t have superhuman abilities, but he’s a highly intelligent criminal mastermind. He commits sadistic crimes with a variety of goofey-thematic props such as: the joker venom and a deck of razor-tipped cards.

His Origin

The Joker doesn't have a definite origin story, although the generally accepted origin one is that he was a criminal formerly known as the Red Hood who got into a confrontation with Batman in a chemical factory and in the process the, falls into the acidic chemical plant that disfigures his skin, turning him into the Joker.
