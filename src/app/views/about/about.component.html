All About Comic Book Characters

Comic book characters are very intriguing.

Be it the heroes' struggles or triumphs, their moral codes, or the villains' their twisted sense of morality, we can, to some extent, relate with these larger-than-life characters. If we can't relate to them, maybe we'll just be fascinated by them.

In this blog, there are character-deep-dives where we study certain aspects of these characters, with examples from comic books and films, what makes them who they are and what we can learn from them.

Hope you enjoy!!

About Me

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the world of fiction, particularly science fiction. I was lucky enough to be introduced into the world of DC and Marvel comic book lore. Primarily, this was through the animated TV shows such as Superman The Animated Series, Batman The Animated Series, Justice League, X-Men Evolution and Batman: The Brave and The Bold (The nostalgia!!!).

Through this, I got to know the characters and most importantly their powers and enemies (simply because at that time, the fights were awesome and the one-liners of the characters witty). Fast forward, there has been a surge of live-action comic book films, from the DCEU and the MCU. This got me interested in reading comics, listening to podcasts on the Hero's Journey and character studies and most importantly, watching the films themselve

My name is Clinton Mutinda, and I love comics.
